Project overview

In this project, we're leveraging the capabilities of an ESP32-S3 microcontroller along with the BQ76942 battery balancer IC to monitor and manage battery cell voltages effectively. The ESP32-S3, known for its powerful processing capabilities and built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, serves as the brain of our system, enabling seamless communication and control.

The BQ76942 battery balancer IC plays a crucial role in ensuring the health and safety of our battery pack. With its advanced features, including precise voltage monitoring and balancing, it helps maintain uniform cell voltages across the battery pack, thus extending its lifespan and maximizing performance.

By integrating these components, our project aims to create a reliable and efficient battery management system suitable for various applications, such as electric vehicles, renewable energy storage systems, and portable electronics. Through real-time monitoring and intelligent balancing, we can optimize the utilization of battery resources while safeguarding against overcharging, over-discharging, and cell imbalances, ensuring a safer and more sustainable energy solution.
